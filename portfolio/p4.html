Typically, I can begin work within 1-2 weeks of the initial agreement. For urgent projects, I may be able to accommodate faster start times depending on my current workload.

Yes, I offer various maintenance packages to keep your website or application running smoothly after launch. These can be customized based on your specific needs.

For most projects, I work with a 50% upfront deposit and the remaining 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can establish milestone-based payments to distribute costs more evenly.

Do you sign NDAs for confidential projects?

Absolutely. I'm happy to sign Non-Disclosure Agreements to protect your intellectual property and ensure confidentiality throughout our collaboration.
